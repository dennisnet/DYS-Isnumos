Features/ Glyphicons

420 Glyphicons at your disposal

Grove includes the Glyphicons Pro package, which also includes the font version. All icons in Grove are Glyphicons and are easily customizable in color, size and appearance. You can switch any icon in Grove simply by switching a CSS class in your HTML markup.

Take the feature boxes on the left side for example. Switch the icons in the box by changing the class of the <i> element from
